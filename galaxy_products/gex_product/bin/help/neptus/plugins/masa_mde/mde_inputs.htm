MASA MDE Models: MDE input formats

Both the MDE training and analysis phases are applied to datasets of vectors of Inline Tests, possibly measured at a number of different locations on a wafer. Datasets must be provided in any of the following native test formats:

  • STDF V3.0
  • STDF V4.0
  • PCM from UMC foundry
  • WAT from TSMC foundry

Extractors or converters for other formats can be developed if necessary. Please contact support.mde@masagroup.net.
