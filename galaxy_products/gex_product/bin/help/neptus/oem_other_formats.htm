Processing Non-STDF test data

Your Examinator release is an OEM version of Quantix Examinator and as such has some restrictions. One restriction is its support for STDF data format only. However if you upgrade to the Quantix Examinator product, you'll be able to analyze any data type including:

  • ATDF

  • TXT / CSV

  • Foundry: WAT and PCM formats

  • GDF Agilent HP93000 and HP83000 formats

  • Compressed formats: .Zip, .Z, .gz

  • etc...

For more information about upgrading your Examinator software, contact Quantix sales at quantix_sales@mentor.com
