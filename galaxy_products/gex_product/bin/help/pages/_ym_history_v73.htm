GEXYM-94 P7968: SYA calculation with bin exclusion Paid: custom SYA Calculation with Bin Exclusion for Vishay The user does not want to change the yield calculations that are standard today. he wants to make a special yield, syl, and sbl calculation such that certain bins are excluded. Today we do:
nb_parts_good / nb_parts
and
nb_parts_in_bin/nb_parts The request is for:
nb_parts_good/(nb_parts - nb_parts_FromExcludedBadBins)
and
nb_parts_in_bin/(nb_parts - nb_parts_FromExcludedBadBins) This calculation would be used for the statistical yield and bin limits and for the calculated yield and bin yield to be used with the statistical yield and bin limits. This is a paid request. Vishay wants to see the real yield and the yield that excludes yields losses in assembly (external vendor). QA suggestions:
Click compute button to export the computation results to file. Export Q objects in export tree in debug mode to make it accessible.
