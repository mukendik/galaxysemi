Examinator's Mission

Quantix Examinator is a comprehensive product line to allow easy Data Analysis and IC Yield Enhancement on your semiconductor data. This solution accepts all your data files (STDF, ATDF, WAT, etc) and generates HTML Web ready reports. Its blazing speed crunches the biggest database in seconds - way faster than any other solution on the market! With Quantix Examinator, you will experience:

  • Blazing speed: no matter how complex the data processing task, your HTML reports are generated instantly!

  • Ease of use: neither overwhelming menus nor long learning curve: all reports are pre-configured (canned reports), so all you have to do is select the report you want!
