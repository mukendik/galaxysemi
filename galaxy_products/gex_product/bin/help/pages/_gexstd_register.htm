Ensure you are a Registered User !

 

Ensure you are a registered user so you receive periodic hints from the Quantix Examinator developer team! To maximize the usage of this great solution, there is nothing best than becoming a registered user and belong to the large community of Examinator users!

If you have downloaded the Quantix Examinator from our web site, and if you have entered
your real name, company name, and company email, then you are already registered. In all
other cases, you need to send us an Email with all the information listed below.

You should register if:

  • You received this software by Email (no matter who sent it, even Quantix personnel)
  • You downloaded this software from a company server, FTP account, or any location
    other than the Quantix web site
  • You received this software pre-installed on your tester, or computer
  • You received a CD-ROM, floppy, or other media that included the software
  • Your email has changed, or you moved to a different company

In all such cases, you need to send us a short email. Simply follow these steps:

  1. Send your email to: support@mentor.com 
  2. Set the subject to: Registration
  3. In the body of the email, provide the following information:
    Last name: <Your last name>
    First name: <Your first name>
    Company: <Your company name>
    Email: <Your company email>
  4. Send the Email.
    You are now a Registered User!

Still not sure if you need to register? Then let us know your exact situation and we will
help you clarify what needs to be done.
