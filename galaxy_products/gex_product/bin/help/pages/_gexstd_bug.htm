Reporting a bug

Quantix Semiconductor is committed to making the Quantix Examinator your favourite data processing solution. Therefore, should you identify any bugs, please contact the support team and give a clear description of the problem. DO NOT send your data files that recreate the problem. Please wait for the Quantix support team to contact you to provide instructions to FTP the file to a private account. For any bug issues, use the email address support@mentor.com 

 

Requesting an enhancement

We want this solution to provide the exceptional features and usability our customers have come to expect from Quantix software. If you have any suggestions to improve the Examinator solution, please forward your comments/requests to the Quantix support team at support@mentor.com 

 

More support

Also, visit the Quantix web site www.mentor.com  where you will find the latest info about new releases and new features.
