Upgrade to Teradyne Examinator-Pro+

Teradyne Examinator-Pro+ extends the capabilities of Examinator in a number of valuable ways, including access to advanced characterization tools, the capability to store and query test data in a relational database and the ability to process data from both Teradyne and non-Teradyne test equipment. Please contact your local Teradyne representative for more information on license upgrades. Thank you.
